Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

🔒 Short version: ConvertSnap converts your images entirely inside your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers. We don't store, process, or transmit your photos anywhere.

1. Information We Collect

ConvertSnap is a client-side web application. Image conversion happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and JavaScript. We do not collect, receive, or store the contents of any files you convert.

We may collect standard web analytics data, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General browser type and operating system
  • Referring URLs and geographic region (country level)

This data is aggregated and never linked to individual identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any analytics data collected is used solely to understand site performance, improve the tool, and ensure reliability. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Advertising

ConvertSnap displays advertisements to support free access to the tool. These ads may be served by third-party networks (such as Google AdSense). Ad networks may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing activity. You can opt out of personalized ads through:

  • Google Ad Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

Third-party ad networks have their own privacy policies which govern how they use data. We encourage you to review those policies.

4. Cookies

ConvertSnap itself does not use first-party cookies beyond what is strictly necessary for site operation. Third-party ad networks and analytics providers may set their own cookies as described in their policies.

5. Your Files Stay on Your Device

This is the most important point: files you upload for conversion are processed entirely on your device. No image data is transmitted to ConvertSnap servers. Once you close your browser tab, all file data is cleared from memory.

6. Children's Privacy

ConvertSnap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will note the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of ConvertSnap after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email: privacy@convertsnap.app
